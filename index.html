02 Work

A field app for a real-estate team.

Built in 12 weeks. Replaced a WhatsApp + paper workflow with one iPhone app used daily.

Case 01 Real estate · Geneva

Where are my people, and what did they see on site?

A real-estate company was running its field team over WhatsApp, photos, and handwritten notes. Reports got lost. Nobody knew who was at which building.

We built a native app for iOS and Android: live location, a per-property task list, and structured reports with photos, all syncing into a web dashboard for the office.

03 Approach

From first call to live product
in three stages.

No lengthy discovery phases with no code. You see something real in week two and every week after.

01

Scope & shape

We meet, we dig into the real problem, we write down exactly what we're building. You get a fixed price and timeline before anything else.

02

Design & build

We design the interface and write the code in short iterations. Every week you get a working version you can try. No black boxes.

03

Launch & support

We ship it to the App Store, the web, or wherever it needs to live. Then we stay on, for fixes, updates, and the features users ask for.
